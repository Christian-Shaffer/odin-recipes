The Best Pizza

It's just the most beautiful pizza on the planet. Crispy, pepperoni, cheesy, goodness.

But how do we make it? Let's find out.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Red sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Onions and other random stuff you like
  • General seasonings (I love red pepper flakes)

How to make it

  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. Roll dough
  3. Spread sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add onions or other fun ingredients
  6. Add seasonings
  7. Put in oven for a lil bit, who cares about the time?
  8. Take out at some point and eat it up
