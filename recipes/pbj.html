Mom's PBJ

Everyone remembers the smacking PBJ your mom would give you as a kid.

How do you ruin the memory? By making it yourself.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Optional: honey

How to make it

  1. Put two slices of bread on a plate
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread jelly on the other
  4. Optional: add some honey on both sides
  5. Combine the two slices
  6. Eat it up
