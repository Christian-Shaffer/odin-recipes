Fantastic Ramen

Do you love how delicious that ramen looks? Well, this recipe is for you!

Ours won't look that great or taste that great, but I do have your attention now.

So let's dive in.

Ingredients

  • Packet of ramen you can get from the dollar store or wherever
  • Water
  • That's probably it.

How to make it

  1. Boil water on stove
  2. While the water is heating up, open the ramen packet
  3. Take out the seasoning packet
  4. Cut the seasoning packet open and set it down
  5. When water is ready, add it to the noodles
  6. Pour seasoning packet contents in container
  7. Wait for it to cool down and eat it up
